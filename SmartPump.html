About Us

90% reduction of size

Simpler, cheaper and quicker installation

Aesthetically superior

SmartPump.ai is a project that aims to help authorities reach their Net Zero goals by bringing emerging technologies to public infrastructure. Their first product will use aerospace-inspired 3D-printed axial flow heat exchanger technology to increase the efficiency and decrease the form factor of domestic air source heat pumps by 80%, making them more feasible and viable for installation in existing housing stock. 

This solution of creating a small, light, efficient, easy-to-install, and aesthetically pleasing Outdoor Unit, enables widespread adoption and allowing for installation in terraced housing for the first time.
